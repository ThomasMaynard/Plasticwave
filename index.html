Plastic Wave

By D’Andre Thompson, Thomas Maynard, and Nia Antoine

New Haven CT, 2023

Dragonframe, Youtube, VSDC Video Editor, Construction Paper, Glue, Plastic, Circuit Board

Our message: Don’t be lazy and recycle your plastics instead of dumping them in the ocean.

Intended Effect: To help viewers visualize and understand why water pollution is bad for the environment.

Importance of this topic: Water pollution affects the health of the water life in the ocean.
